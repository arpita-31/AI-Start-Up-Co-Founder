## 📖 Overview

**AI Startup Co-Founder** is an intelligent multi-agent platform that helps entrepreneurs transform startup ideas into actionable business strategies.

Instead of spending weeks conducting market research, analyzing competitors, planning MVP features, creating business plans, and preparing investor presentations, users can simply enter their startup idea and receive a complete startup blueprint generated by AI.

The platform acts as a virtual co-founder, assisting founders through every stage of startup planning—from idea validation to investor readiness.

🎯 Problem Statement

Many startup founders struggle with:

- Validating business ideas
- Understanding market opportunities
- Identifying competitors
- Defining MVP scope
- Planning execution strategies
- Creating investor presentations
- Building professional business plans

These activities often require significant time, expertise, and resources.

---

💡 Solution

AI Startup Co-Founder automates startup planning through a team of specialized AI agents.

Each agent performs a dedicated business function and collaborates with other agents to generate comprehensive startup documentation and strategic insights.

The result is a complete startup planning workflow that transforms a simple idea into an investor-ready business package.
